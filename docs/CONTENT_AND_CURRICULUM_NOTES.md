# Content and curriculum notes

## Public terminology

Use “Starting Point,” “Starting-Point Activity,” “学习起点” and “数学起点活动.” Avoid “placement test,” “分级测试,” “能力测试” or any phrasing that implies diagnosis, ranking or a formal assessment.

Equal groups, fair sharing, rows and counting by twos are presented as optional extension content. MathSprout does not claim multiplication-table instruction.

## Claims boundary

The site does not claim that MathSprout:

- diagnoses ability;
- replaces kindergarten teaching;
- guarantees academic improvement;
- is officially curriculum-aligned, endorsed or certified;
- has completed user testing;
- has verified every live interaction or device.
